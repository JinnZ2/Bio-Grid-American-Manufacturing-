- **Wave energy concentration**: New bays and channels focus wave energy
- **Tidal amplification**: Changed coastal shapes amplify tidal energy

- **Vegetation transition energy**: Salt marsh and mangrove bioelectric changes
- **Soil chemistry energy**: Salt intrusion creates chemical energy gradients
- **Root system adaptation energy**: Plant bioelectric patterns during salt adaptation

## Complete System Architecture

### Four-Layer Integrated Energy Harvesting

#### Layer 1: Sediment Energy Harvesting (0-50m depth)

**Sediment Transport Mechanical Energy**

- **Technology**: Piezoelectric arrays embedded in strategic sediment transport pathways
- **Mechanism**: Moving sediments generate pressure on piezoelectric materials → electricity
- **Placement**: Underwater sediment channels where eroded material flows during ocean rise events
- **Output**: 20-80 MW depending on sediment transport volume

**Sediment Deposition Energy**

```
Sediment_Energy = Mass_Transported × Velocity² × Piezoelectric_Efficiency × Channel_Coverage

Where:
- Mass_Transported: 10⁶-10⁹ tons/year during major ocean rise events
- Velocity: 0.5-5 m/s in sediment transport channels
- Piezoelectric_Efficiency: 15-25% mechanical to electrical conversion
- Channel_Coverage: Percentage of sediment flow captured (target: 60-80%)
```

**Chemical Energy from Sediment**

- **Source**: Electrochemical reactions as minerals in transported sediment interact with seawater
- **Technology**: Bioelectrochemical cells integrated with sediment deposition areas
- **Output**: 5-15 MW continuous from chemical gradients

#### Layer 2: Salinity Gradient Energy (Surface to 20m depth)

**Reverse Electrodialysis (RED) Systems**

- **Technology**: Membrane-based systems harvesting energy from fresh/salt water mixing
- **Advantage**: Ocean rise creates massive new mixing zones with high energy potential
- **Configuration**: Floating and fixed membrane arrays in estuaries and mixing zones

```
Salinity_Power = Membrane_Area × Current_Density × Voltage_Differential × System_Efficiency

Where:
- Membrane_Area: 10⁴-10⁶ m² depending on mixing zone size
- Current_Density: 2-8 A/m² (enhanced in optimal salinity gradients)
- Voltage_Differential: 0.1-0.3 V per membrane pair
- System_Efficiency: 85-95% for optimized RED systems
```

**Osmotic Pressure Energy**

- **Technology**: Pressure retarded osmosis (PRO) systems
- **Mechanism**: Natural osmotic pressure from salinity gradients drives turbines
- **Output**: 30-120 MW from large estuary installations

**Enhanced Coastal Mixing Energy**

- **Source**: Ocean rise creates new river/ocean mixing configurations
- **Technology**: Optimized channel design concentrates mixing energy
- **Output**: 15-50 MW from enhanced natural mixing processes

#### Layer 3: Hydrodynamic Energy Enhancement (All depths)

**Wave Energy Concentration**

- **Strategy**: Design protective coastal infrastructure that concentrates rather than dissipates wave energy

- **Innovation**: Coastal protection that generates energy instead of consuming it

```
Wave_Power_Enhanced = Traditional_Wave_Power × Concentration_Factor × Protection_Efficiency

- Traditional_Wave_Power: 10-50 MW from normal wave energy systems
```

**Tidal Amplification Energy**

**Ocean Current Enhancement**

#### Layer 4: Ecosystem Transition Energy (Coastal zone)
